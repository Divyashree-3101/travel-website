Know About Us

At HappyTravels, we believe in making travel experiences memorable.

Coorg

Coorg, officially known as Kodagu, is a hill station in Karnataka known for its lush greenery.

Jaipur

Jaipur, the Pink City, is famous for its palaces, forts, and rich culture.

Ooty

Ooty, Tamil Nadu, is known for its lakes, viewpoints, and tea plantations.

Munnar

Munnar, a picturesque hill station in Kerala, is famed for its sprawling tea plantations, misty mountains, and serene valleys, making popular tourist spots like Mattupetty Dam, Pothamedu Viewpoint, Kundala Lake, Anamudi Peak, and Rajamalai National Park

Ladakh

Ladakh has many tourist places, including monasteries, lakes, palaces, and wildlife sanctuaries.

Darjeeling

Darjeeling, often called the "Queen of Hills," is a popular tourist destination in India, renowned for its breathtaking views of the Himalayas, sprawling tea gardens, and the iconic Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.
